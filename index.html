Who I Am

I'm a cybersecurity professional completing my M.Sc. in Computer Science at Saarland University, with thesis research conducted at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security under Prof. Dr. Thorsten Holz.

At Allianz Technology SE, I own dual-jurisdiction NIS2 incident reporting playbooks for Germany (BSI) and Italy (ACN), build Power Automate workflows for alert triage, and contribute to DORA gap analysis on the Protection & Resilience team.

My background spans AppSec (RESTler fuzzing in CI/CD pipelines at Bosch), Cloud Security (Azure IoT TARA under ISO/SAE 21434), and academic security research (privacy-preserving authentication, fraud detection). My long-term goal is a CISO-track role.
